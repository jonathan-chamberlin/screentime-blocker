Frequently Asked Questions

Open the dashboard at localhost:3456 and click Lock In. Your work timer starts immediately and blocked sites become inaccessible.
The timer counts time you spend on productive sites in your browser or using productive desktop apps. Time on blocked or unrelated sites doesn't count. Once you hit your work goal (default: 50 minutes), you earn a break.
Blocked sites are sites you can't visit during a work session. Go to Settings → Lists, open a list, and add domains under Blocked Sites (e.g. youtube.com).
Confetti fires and you earn break time (default: 10 minutes). During your break, blocked sites become temporarily accessible. When break time runs out, they're blocked again automatically.
After you've spent time on a site during a session, the app checks in to confirm you're actually working there. Click Yes, I'm working to approve the site — it won't ask again. Click No, block this site to add it to your blocked list instantly.
In Strict Mode, you cannot end your session early or adjust settings while a session is active. It's designed for people who need hard accountability with no escape hatches.
If you end a session without hitting your work goal, you can be prompted to donate to a charity (or pay someone) as a consequence. Configure the charity, amount, and payment method in Settings → Penalty Configuration.
